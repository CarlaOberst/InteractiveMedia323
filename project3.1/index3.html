This website explores three texts: The House of Asterion by Jorge Luis Borges, House of Leaves by Mark Z Danielewski, and In Deep: The Dark and Dangerous World of Extreme Cavers by Burkhard Bilger. On each page, there will be at least one place within the text that you can click to travel to another page. The connections meander, circling back on themselves infinitely, with no set ending. Don't worry about exploring every choice that a page might present at once; you will almost certainly find yourself returning to that page again at some point.
